A simple custom menu for the Adjudication core to discuss motions, implemented while learning Google App Scripts.

https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1fxUSIAdbQskH0dS3nu3-ldVSPjD0lfzXo5cj2ZluzwU/edit?usp=sharing

Notes:

  1. Click on the link. It will open a google spreadsheet. Sign in to your google account, if you are already not. Click on "File" and then on "Make a copy". This will give you editing rights. Give it a whirl.
  2. Wrap is set true to all cells on Open. Just in case it doesn't wrap after you've entered text, refresh the page.
  3. Select the cell which contains the motion and choose the appropriate menu option.
  4. The sortedMotions sheet will have the motions sorted to the category you chose, and the row that contains the motion will be coloured appropriately.
